Blackforest Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 50g salted butter, softened
  • 2tbsp powdered sugar
  • 2tbsp cocoa powder
  • 100g white flour
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk, 1/2 cup duke soda
  • 1/2tsp soda, 1tsp baking powder
  • 1tsp Vanilla essence, cherries
  • Whipped cream, Grated chocolate

Method

  • Grease the cake tin.
  • Sieve cocoa powder, baking powder, soda, flour 3 times in a bowl.
  • Take condensed milk, powder sugar, and butter in a bowl and beat till sugar dissolves.
  • Add 2/3 drops essence - mix well, add dry ingredients and stir properly.
  • Add liquid soda and swift.
  • Pour batter into greased tin and bake in a pre heated oven for 25 to 30 minutes.
  • Decorate the cake with grated chocolate and cherries.
